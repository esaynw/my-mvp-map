About this project

This map visualizes reported bike accidents and cycling infrastructure in Montreal using open data from the City of Montreal. Each point represents a collision event, which can be filtered by color-coded severity, speed, and other,while lines indicate types of bike lanes.

The goal is to support safer mobility by identifying hotspots and highlighting the relationship between cycling networks and safety outcomes.
